### App Description

#### Short Description (80 characters max)

#### Full Description
RideNow Taxi is your professional ride-booking companion for quick, safe, and affordable transportation. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or exploring the city, RideNow connects you with licensed taxi drivers in seconds.

**Key Features:**
• **Real-time Tracking**: Watch your driver approach on the map
• **Upfront Pricing**: See your fare before you book
• **Multiple Payment Options**: Pay with card, cash, or digital wallet
• **Driver Ratings**: Rate your ride and help improve service quality
• **24/7 Support**: Get help whenever you need it
• **Trip History**: Access your past rides anytime
• **Multiple Vehicle Types**: Choose from economy, comfort, or premium rides

**Why Choose RideNow?**
✓ Licensed, professional drivers
✓ Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
✓ GPS-tracked routes for safety
✓ Easy booking in 3 simple steps
✓ Available 24/7 in major cities

**How It Works:**
1. Enter your pickup and drop-off locations
2. Choose your ride type and see the fare
3. Confirm booking and track your driver in real-time

Download RideNow Taxi today and experience the future of urban mobility!
