# Privacy Policy - RideNow Taxi

**Last Updated: January 6, 2026**

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- Name, email address, and phone number
- Profile photo (if provided)
- Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

### Location Information
- Current location for ride matching
- Pickup and destination addresses
- Real-time location during active rides

### Usage Information
- Ride history and preferences
- App usage statistics
- Device information and crash reports

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Our Services
- Match you with available drivers
- Calculate fares and route distances
- Process payments securely
- Provide customer support

### To Improve Our Services
- Analyze usage patterns
- Optimize app performance
- Develop new features

### For Safety and Security
- Verify user identities
- Monitor for fraudulent activity
- Ensure safe ride experiences

## Data Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:
- With drivers for ride completion
- With payment processors for transactions
- With law enforcement when required by law
- With service providers who help operate our app

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure payment processing
- Regular security audits
- Data access controls

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Request data portability

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
